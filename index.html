Land Prices Per Perch in Sri Lanka 2025 — Full Guide

PriceIn.com.lk is Sri Lanka's most-searched price reference portal. We publish land prices per perch, house prices, vehicle prices, and gold rates across all 25 districts. Our land price guide is the most comprehensive freely available resource for checking property market values from Colombo to Jaffna.

Land Price Per Perch — Western Province

The Western Province holds Sri Lanka's highest land values. In premium Colombo areas like Cinnamon Gardens (Colombo 7), Kollupitiya (Colombo 3), and Bambalapitiya (Colombo 4), land prices range from LKR 3.5 million to over 6 million per perch. Suburban areas like Nugegoda, Maharagama, Battaramulla and Malabe command LKR 1.2M–2.5M per perch. In Gampaha district, Wattala and Peliyagoda are rising fast, while outer towns like Nittambuwa and Mirigama offer land from LKR 150,000–350,000 per perch.

Land Price Per Perch — Kandy & Central Province

Kandy is Sri Lanka's second most active property market. Land prices in Kandy City centre range from LKR 1M–2.5M per perch. Surrounding areas like Peradeniya, Katugastota, and Pallekele are highly sought after by families and investors. Dambulla in Matale district is growing rapidly due to expressway access and commercial activity, with land from LKR 300,000–700,000 per perch.

Land Price Per Perch — Southern Province (Galle, Matara, Hambantota)

The southern coast has seen dramatic price increases due to tourism. Land prices in Hikkaduwa, Unawatuna and Mirissa range from LKR 700,000–1.8M per perch for beach-access plots. Galle City is in high demand for both residential and commercial development. Hambantota District, benefiting from port and airport infrastructure, is an emerging investment destination.

Vehicle Prices in Sri Lanka 2025

The most popular vehicles in Sri Lanka by search volume are the Toyota Aqua (LKR 7.5M–9.5M), Toyota Prius (LKR 9M–12M), Bajaj Three-Wheel (LKR 900K–1.2M), and Suzuki Alto (LKR 4.8M–5.5M). Prices vary significantly based on year, condition, and prevailing import duties. Three-wheel (tuk-tuk) prices are a major search category in Sri Lanka and we list both brand-new and used rates.

Gold Price Sri Lanka Today

Sri Lanka gold prices are quoted per gram for 18K, 22K, and 24K gold. As of April 2025, 22K gold is approximately LKR 24,850 per gram and 24K is around LKR 27,200 per gram. Sovereign (8g) prices hover around LKR 198,000. Rates fluctuate daily based on the international gold price and USD/LKR exchange rate.
